Just a few facts about plastic pollution:
·We currently recover only 5% of the plastics we produce.
·50% of the plastic we use, we use just once and throw away.
·The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is the largest ocean garbage site in the world, a floating mass of plastic two times bigger than Texas.
·One million sea birds and 100.000 marine mammals are killed annually from plastic in our oceans.
