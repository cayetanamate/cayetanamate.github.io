Hazlo Tú

ILLUSTRATION

This is my proposal for the design contest "Hazlo Tú" promoted by Yorokobu, a Spanish magazine, in partnership with Volkswagen.

In my proposal, the cover represents a map of the city, where the letters of Yorokobu are part of the architecture, a space with green areas and lakes, and on the back cover the Volkswagen logo transforms in a roundabout.
