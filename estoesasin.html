Esto es asin

ILLUSTRATION & TYPOGRAPHY

An illustrated list of Spanish words that are now accepted by the Royal Spanish Academy, some of them have been changed over time and others are just new.

I have illustrated 18 words trying to create a harmony between them, using a same illustration style, flat design.

Words list: Albericoque, Almóndiga, Apartotel, Bluyín, Cederrón, Chupi, Dotor, Espanglish, Güisqui, Jonrón, Murciégalo, Nocaut, Norabuena, Otubre, Pompis, Sunami, Toballa and Vagamundo.
